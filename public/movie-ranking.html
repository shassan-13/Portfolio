The Best Movies According to Hassan

My Top 3 movies of all time

The Life is beautiful

'The Life is beautiful' ranks first in my list. I like this movie very much. The movie describes the beautiful bonding between father and son.

The pursuit of happiness

'The pursuit of happiness' ranks second in my list. The movie describes the struggle of a man's life and how he becomes successful. I like the climax, which is so emotional.

Interstellar

'Interstellar' ranks third in my list. This movie was directed by Christopher Nolan. My favorite director of all time. The movie is based on space and science stuff.
